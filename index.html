COVID-19 and Healthcare Workers

Total Employment

Total US employment falls sharply from March to April 2020, and has slowly recovered (though not completely as of January 2022). The data is shown as a percentage change relative to January 2020.

Sector

These overall trends are mirrored in the Health Care and Social Assistance sector.

Disaggregating the sector: Ambulatory Care

Ambulatory Care includes doctor's offices, dentists, and optometrists. The line still mirrors the general trend seen in the total US labor market.

Disaggregating the sector: Hospitals

In contrast, hospital employment did not drop as dramatically between March and April 2020, but has decreased overall compared to January 2020.

Disaggregating the sector: Nursing and Residential Care

Another outlier is Nursing and Residential Care: the trend shows a clear decreasing trend as the pandemic has progressed. What are the reasons for this trend?
